Cookies

This is my absolute favourite recipe for cookies! Just like grandma used to make them...

You'll learn the best way to make classic chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 120g butter, softened
  • 75g light brown sugar
  • 75g golden caster sugar
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 180g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 150g dark chocolate , cut into chunks
